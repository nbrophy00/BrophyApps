DELETE ACCOUNT

1. Delete Your SongBank Account

1.1 Overview

SongBank provides an in-app option to delete your account and associated data. This page explains how to request deletion and what happens after your request is submitted.

  • • You can delete your account directly inside the SongBank mobile app.
  • • Account deletion removes your stored SongBank data from our active systems.
  • • Some limited data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
  • • Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store / Google Play and must be canceled there if you want to stop billing.

1.2 How to Delete Your Account In-App

To delete your SongBank account from within the app:

Step 1: Open the SongBank app and sign in.

Step 2: Go to Settings.

Step 3: Select Account Management.

Step 4: Review the information shown and confirm deletion.

Once confirmed, your account deletion request will be processed and you will be signed out.

1.3 What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, SongBank deletes the data associated with your account from our active systems. This may include:

  • • Your SongBank account profile (authentication identifier).
  • • Imported royalty statement data you uploaded to SongBank.
  • • Any artwork links and catalog metadata saved in the app.
  • • App configuration data tied to your account (such as preferences and ordering).

1.4 Retention Timeline

After you confirm deletion in the app:

  • • Your SongBank account and associated data are removed from our active systems as part of the deletion process.
  • • Backups (if applicable) are fully cycled out within 90 days.

1.5 Subscription Billing (Important)

Deleting your SongBank account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. Subscriptions are managed by the platform where you purchased them:

  • • If you subscribed on iOS, manage/cancel in your Apple App Store subscriptions.
  • • If you subscribed on Android, manage/cancel in Google Play subscriptions.

1.6 If You Can’t Access the App

If you cannot access the SongBank app (for example, you no longer have access to the email you used to sign in), you can request account deletion by contacting support:

Email: support@songbank-app.com

Please include the email address associated with your SongBank account and the words “Delete Account Request” in the subject line. For your security, we may request additional verification before processing deletion.

2. Contact Information

For questions or concerns, contact us at support@songbank-app.com.
