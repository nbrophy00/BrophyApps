ABOUT

1. What is SongBank?

SongBank is a mobile app that helps artists, songwriters, and rights holders track, understand, and analyze music royalty income across multiple sources — all in one place.

Instead of logging into separate platforms like distributors, PROs, and publishers, SongBank allows you to:

  • • Import royalty statements from your various income sources ("Banks")
  • • View earnings by song, source, and time period
  • • Track catalog performance over time
  • • Estimate catalog value using industry-standard multiples

SongBank turns fragmented royalty data into a clear, organized financial picture of your music catalog.

2. How SongBank Works

SongBank works by importing your official royalty statements from each of your income sources. Each source is represented as a "Bank" inside the app (e.g., distributors, PROs, publishing admins, etc.).

Once imported, SongBank:

  • • Groups earnings by song
  • • Calculates totals and trends
  • • Identifies top-performing sources
  • • Displays your catalog in a unified dashboard

3. Choosing the Correct Statement Type

To ensure your data is accurate and consistent, it's important to download the correct version of your royalty statements.

Always choose: Reported Date (Not Sales Date)

When given the option, always download statements using "Reported Date."

Why this matters:

  • • "Reported Date" reflects when revenue is actually paid and recognized
  • • "Sales Date" can create inconsistencies across sources
  • • Using "Reported Date" keeps all your data aligned across Banks

4. File Format: CSV or XLSX Only

When downloading statements, always select:

  • • CSV (.csv), or
  • • Excel (.xlsx)

These formats provide the most detailed and structured data, allowing SongBank to:

  • • Accurately parse earnings
  • • Match songs correctly
  • • Calculate totals and analytics

Avoid: PDF statements

  • • PDFs are not structured for reliable data extraction
  • • They often result in incomplete or unusable data

5. Manual Entry Banks

Some income sources do not provide downloadable statements in a structured format. For these, SongBank supports manual transaction entry.

Manual Entry Required For:

  • • Resin8
  • • SAG-AFTRA
  • • Pipeline

These sources typically:

  • • Do not issue consistent digital statements, or
  • • Provide formats that cannot be reliably imported

How it works:

  • • Navigate to the Bank inside SongBank
  • • Tap the upload / add entry option
  • • Enter transaction details manually

6. Best Practices for Accurate Tracking

To get the most accurate results in SongBank:

  • • Always use Reported Date statements
  • • Use CSV or XLSX formats only
  • • Import statements regularly (monthly or quarterly)
  • • Ensure song titles are consistent across sources when possible
  • • Add album artwork to improve catalog visibility

7. Why This Matters

Music income comes from many different places — each with its own format, timing, and reporting method. SongBank standardizes this data so you can:

  • • Understand your true earnings
  • • Identify growth trends
  • • Make smarter catalog decisions
  • • Present your catalog professionally

8. Getting Started

  • • Choose a Bank (e.g., Amplified, ASCAP, BMI, etc.)
  • • Download your statement (Reported Date, CSV/XLSX)
  • • Import into SongBank
  • • View your catalog instantly
