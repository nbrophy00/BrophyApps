SUPPORT

1. Frequently Asked Questions

What is SongBank?

SongBank is a music-royalty aggregation and catalog-valuation app that helps you track income from multiple royalty sources, analyze trends over time, and estimate the value of your catalog in one place.

Who is SongBank for?

SongBank is built for songwriters, artists, producers, publishers, and rights holders who receive music royalties and want a clearer picture of how their catalog is performing.

What platforms does SongBank support?

SongBank is available for iOS and Android. Your data is stored securely on your device and can sync to the cloud so you can access it across devices.

Which royalty sources and file types can I import?

SongBank supports CSV, TSV, and XLSX statements from major royalty providers such as ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, SoundExchange, the MLC, Music Reports, DistroKid, TuneCore, OneRPM, and more.

What happens if I accidentally import the same statement twice?

SongBank checks each file using a unique fingerprint (MD5 hash). If a statement has already been imported, it is automatically detected and skipped to prevent duplicate earnings.

Why do some very small amounts show as $0.00* instead of $0.00?

When a royalty is greater than zero but less than one cent, SongBank displays it as $0.00* so you can distinguish truly zero earnings from very small but non-zero income.

How does SongBank estimate my catalog value?

SongBank calculates historical earnings and multi-year averages for your songs, then applies valuation multiples commonly used in catalog deals. You can view these estimates for a single song, a group of songs, or your entire catalog.

Can I see trends over time for my catalog?

Yes. SongBank lets you view earnings by month, year, source, and song so you can see how your catalog is performing over time and which titles or income streams are driving the most value.

How does album artwork matching work?

SongBank uses your song’s title formatting and optional Spotify links (or IDs such as ISRC/UPC) to match and display album artwork. For the most accurate results, enter the song title exactly as it appears in the app when adding or editing artwork.

Can I change the album artwork for a song?

Yes. On the Edit Artwork screen you can paste a new Spotify link to update the artwork. The title itself is kept locked so that artwork continues to match the correct SongBank entry.

Does SongBank work offline?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device, so you can view your catalog and past statements offline. When you reconnect, changes can sync with the cloud if sync is enabled.

How does SongBank keep my data secure?

SongBank uses secure, encrypted connections for sync and relies on trusted authentication (such as Firebase Auth) for login. Your password is never stored by SongBank itself.

What types of notifications can I receive?

SongBank can notify you when new royalty statements are imported, when key catalog metrics change, or when certain in-app events occur. You can manage these notifications in your device and app settings.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play Store on your device. From there you can change plans, update payment methods, or cancel at any time. If you reinstall the app, use “Restore Purchases” to refresh your access.
